About Me

Hello, I'm Meir Goldberger, the CEO of Veeshift. With a passion for improving healthcare staffing, I lead a technology-driven platform dedicated to connecting skilled healthcare providers with licensed facilities. Our goal is to ensure quality care and accountability in nursing homes, making work life easier for administrators and schedulers.

My Vision

At Veeshift, our vision is to revolutionize the healthcare staffing industry by providing a customizable and efficient platform that meets the unique needs of each facility. We strive to maintain the highest standards of quality and accountability, ensuring that every shift is filled with competent and reliable healthcare professionals.

Our platform is designed with the end-user in mind, offering features such as automatic shift request approvals, electronic signature timesheets, and a Premium Recruitment Service for targeted hiring. By leveraging technology and maintaining a local focus, we aim to provide unmatched service and support to both providers and facilities.
